Zdog is a no frills Javascript 3D engine that renders with a flat shader and orthographic view. I was interested in seeing if it could be a quick replacement for a project that would have utilized low poly 3-D models. Ultimately Zdog is better suited for building rounded, primitive shapes - still it was quite fun picking up the Zdog library and learning its possibilities and limitations.
