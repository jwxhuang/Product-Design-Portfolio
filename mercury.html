Building a
design system
for Mercury

While working with Est-Rouge and Senkō Labs I got the chance to help establish a design system for their in-house product Mercury. My main goals were to build a cohesive design system that can work cross-functionally as well as streamline dependencies for engineers.

I explored and improved upon designs within the Mercury mobile ecosystem and helped improve the AI model to encourage data feeding and self-training its neural networks.

Tasks: DESIGN SYSTEM • UX/UI DESIGN • PROTOTYPING • VQA

*Because Mercury is still underdevelopment, my work with Mercury is under NDA. Please reach out for more information.

What is Mercury?

Mercury is a smart travel agent that lives in your pocket. Built on top of complex neural networks, Mercury connects travelers with beautiful, carefully curated destinations and helps them book experiences anywhere in the world.
